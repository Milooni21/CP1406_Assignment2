About Us


Petite Treats is one of the famous bakery in the kirwan. We pride ourselves in outstanding true-to the recipes that have been passed from prior years, but also in our ability to create new thrilling cakes to satisfy the customer's requirements.


We lovingly produce each dessert out from the finest ingredients.


We proceed to develop our range of items so they not only seem and taste delightful but also offer nutritional benefits and understand the development towards eating.
